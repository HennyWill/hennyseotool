Privacy Policy

Welcome to our browser extension. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Our browser extension collects the following types of information:

  • Browsing Data: We may collect the URL of the current tab to perform specific functions such as searching, copying headings, highlighting links, and checking for broken links.
  • User Interactions: We track user interactions with the extension, such as button clicks, to provide the requested functionalities.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide the functionalities of the extension, including:

  • Performing searches based on the current site.
  • Copying and highlighting headings, links, and images.
  • Checking for broken links and generating reports.
  • Redirecting to external services like Google Search Console, Ahrefs, and the Internet Archive.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the functionalities mentioned above. Specifically:

  • Redirects to external services are initiated by user actions, and these services may have their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request information about the data we have collected from you.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete any data we have collected from you.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
